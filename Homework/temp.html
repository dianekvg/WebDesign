Latitude vs. Temperature

    This analysis revealed that proximity to the equator is a good indicator that the city will have a somewhat elevated temperature. Temperatures decline as you move farther from the equator, with the decline occuring faster as you move North; the decline in temperatures as you move South are not as dramatic.
